CRM Operating Guide

Standard procedures for managing sponsor relationships

How Sponsor Data Gets Updated

  • Email checks 3× daily — Morning, midday, evening scan of inbox for sponsor replies, contracts, briefs, and payment confirmations.
  • Manual entry — After calls, meetings, or DMs, update the CRM immediately with new info, next actions, and dates.
  • Last Contact Date — Updated automatically when any interaction is logged. Cards go yellow (stale) after 7 days without contact.

When to Advance Stages

TransitionTrigger
Inquiry → NegotiationBrand responds with interest; discussing rates, format, or deliverables.
Negotiation → ContractBoth sides agree on rate and deliverables; contract sent or received.
Contract → ContentContract signed. Brief received or requested. Production begins.
Content → DeliveredFinal content delivered to brand for approval (or no approval needed).
Delivered → LiveVideo published with sponsor integration live.
Live → PaidPayment received and confirmed in bank account.

Payment Tracking Workflow

  1. When video goes live — Set live_date. Payment is typically due 30 days from live date.
  2. Set payment_due_date — Calculate from live_date + payment_terms_days (default 30).
  3. For CPM deals — At 30 days post-live, record views_at_30_days. Calculate final invoice: views ÷ 1000 × CPM rate.
  4. Generate invoice — Send invoice on or before payment_due_date. Set invoice_generated_date.
  5. Follow up — If unpaid 7 days past due, follow up. Card shows red (urgent) when overdue.
  6. Mark paid — Move to "Paid" stage. Set payment_received_date.

Content Phase Workflow

When a deal is in the Content stage, track progress with content_phase:

Brief Pending Brief Received Script Writing Script Submitted Script Approved Recording Editing Review
  • Brief — Request brief from brand contact. Set brief_due date.
  • Script — Write integration script. Submit for brand approval by script_due.
  • Approval — Brand reviews script. Must approve by brand_approval_deadline.
  • Record — Film the integration. Move to Delivered when done.

Card Visual Indicators

  • Red border — Urgent: Overdue payment or overdue next action.
  • Yellow border — Stale: No contact in 7+ days.
  • Blue border — Action needed: Next action due within 3 days.
